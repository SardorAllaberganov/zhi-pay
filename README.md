# ZhiPay

> Send money from Uzbekistan to China in under a minute.

## The problem

Uzbek users — traders, migrant families, tourists, students — need to move UZS to recipients on **Alipay** or **WeChat Pay** in China. Today's options (bank wires, cash exchange, informal couriers) are slow, expensive, and opaque about exchange rates.

## What ZhiPay does

ZhiPay is a mobile-first remittance app. Pay with your **UzCard**, **Humo**, **Visa**, or **Mastercard**; the recipient gets **CNY** in their Alipay or WeChat account. The exchange rate is locked the moment you submit, all fees are visible before you confirm, and the full transfer lifecycle is traceable in-app.

## Who it's for

- **Traders** sourcing goods in Yiwu / Guangzhou — high limits, predictable rate
- **Migrant families** supporting relatives in China — saved recipients, recurring sends
- **Tourists** topping up Alipay / WeChat before travel — one-shot transfer, no friction
- **Students** receiving funds from family — low minimum, occasional use

## Highlights

- **Under 60 seconds** end-to-end from amount entry to "sent"
- **Rate locked at submit** — no surprise FX recalculation mid-flight
- **All four card schemes** — UzCard, Humo, Visa, Mastercard
- **MyID-backed KYC** — Uzbekistan national e-ID, designed for CBU alignment
- **Trilingual** — Uzbek (default), Russian, English
